## 1. Voice Questionnaire

To get started, we need your team to complete the [HOAi Voice Questionnaire]({{questionnaire_link}}). This questionnaire drives your Agent Operating Procedures (AOP) — essentially the playbook that tells your voice agent how to handle calls, transfers, and community-specific questions. The more detailed and timely your responses, the smoother the rest of the process. Delays here directly impact your activation timeline.

If your team is able to complete the questionnaire by **{{questionnaire_deadline}}**, that puts us in a great position to stay on track for your activation date.

## 2. Alignment Call

Upon completing the questionnaire during the first week, let us know and we'll send over availability to schedule your alignment call — typically about an hour. During this call we'll walk through your AOP together, demo the HOAi Voice interface, confirm your transfer setup, and address any open questions.

If we're able to hold this call by **{{alignment_call_deadline}}**, that gives us plenty of runway heading into your activation date.

## 3. Agent Configuration & Internal Testing

After the alignment call, we'll build your custom voice agent based on your AOP. Before handing anything off to your team, we'll run a round of internal testing on our end to validate the agent's configuration.

Once we're confident in the build, we'll provide your team with a phone number to conduct your own internal testing — we call this the Presidential Valley test. This is your team's opportunity to call in and validate caller recognition, balance inquiries, action item creation, community rules, and live transfers to confirm the agent works as expected before any real calls come through.

This will be held the week of **{{internal_testing_week}}**.

## 4. Call Forwarding Setup

Before we can test or go live, your team will need to configure call forwarding with your phone provider. This is one of the most common sources of delay, so I'd encourage your IT or phone contact to begin reviewing the setup early.

If your team uses RingCentral, Microsoft Teams, or Zoom, setup is typically straightforward. If you're on a less common platform, let us know and we can help troubleshoot. Please don't wait until testing week to tackle this.

## 5. Activation Day Live Test

On your activation date of **{{activation_day}}**, we'll enable call forwarding for a 2-hour live orientation window with your staff on standby. This lets us validate the agent's performance with real calls in a controlled window.

We know this is a big change, please know our team is here for you and ready to help you need us.

## 6. Day-Two Adjustments

The following business day, **{{day_two_date}}**, we'll review the data from the live test and make any necessary adjustments to the agent's configuration. Any feedback that your team gathers from the orientation period is greatly appreciated as well.

## 7. Full Deployment & Ongoing Optimization

On business day three, **{{day_three_date}}**, call forwarding is enabled permanently. From there, we'll monitor call data and refine the agent during a 30-day optimization window. Your team can submit adjustments anytime by emailing ask@hoai.com directly to submit a support ticket.
